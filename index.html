I am Redwan Ahmed Rizvee, currently working as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Here you can get a glimpse of myself, what I do and like to do.

I completed my B.Sc and MS from the same department, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Dhaka (CSEDU). In the past I worked as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at East West University from 2022 to 2023. I also worked as a research scientist in TigerIT Bangladesh Limited from 2021 to 2022. I have been an active member of our Data Mining and Machine Learning Research Lab since my undergrad. I am also a part of Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab, working as a Co-Investigator with Dr. Mosaddek Khan. I have completed my B.Sc and MS thesis under the supervision of Dr. Chowdhury Farhan Ahmed. My research interests include Deep Learning, Data Mining, Programming Languages and Systems.

I love to learn new things and think about different research problems. I also love to take photos and watch zillions of movies and series. You can find a glimpse of those here.
