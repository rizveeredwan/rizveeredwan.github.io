Hi, I'm Redwan Ahmed Rizvee.

PhD Student in CS @ UMass Amherst

I study how to make massive datasets interpretable in real-time. As a researcher at the DREAM Lab, advised by Dr. Alexandra Meliou, I develop Perception-Aware Sampling algorithms that bridge statistical learning theory with visual analytics.

Previously, I was a Research Scientist at TigerIT and a Lecturer at the University of Dhaka. I am interested in Data Mining, Database Theory, and building scalable systems.
